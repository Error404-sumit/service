Detailed Day Wise Itinerary

  • Day : 1

    Arrive Jammu , get together and help on arrival and carry on straight to Katra. Arrive Katra and check in at your Hotel. afterward proceed to Vaishnodevi temple. Arrive at Vaishnodevi temple. Clean and change before Mata Darshan. After Mata Vaishnodevi Darshan, go on for Bhairo Ji Darshan (Optional). Overnight stay in Katra Hotel

  • Day : 2

    After Vaishnodevi Mata Darhsan, Arrive at Katra and proceed to Drive Srinagar, , Arrive at Srinagar Check-in Hotel, Overnight Stay in Hotel.

  • Day : 3

    Full day tour to Sonmarg which is the most gorgeous drive from Srinagar. On the manner we stop at a lot of attractive spots. Continue drive to Sonmarg. Sonmarg is known as Gateway of Ladakh. In the evening arrival to Hotel. Overnight Stay in Hotel.

  • Day : 4

    After breakfast go on for half day travel around of world famous Mughal Gardens visiting the Nishat Bagh and Shalimar Bagh (Adobe of love). come back to hotel for lunch. Also get a view of several very beautiful places around the Lake. In the evening Shikara trip on Dal Lake to enjoy the sunset. After tourism back & Overnight Stay in Hotel.

  • Day : 5

    In the morning proceed for full day visit of Gulmarg which is one of the most attractive summer resort in the valley. Enjoy the majesty of nature and the snowy mountains. One can have Gondola travel from Gulmarg to Khalinmarg. In the evening come back to Hotel and comfortable Overnight stay in Gulmarg.

  • Day : 6

    In the Morning after breakfast depart for Jammu to take the train/flight for onward journey.
